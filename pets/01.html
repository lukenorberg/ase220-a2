George

Breed:
British Shorthair

Sex:
Male

Spayed/Neutered:
Yes

George enjoys leisurely days, lounging in sunny spots, and watching the world go by. He's not overly demanding for attention but appreciates a good chin scratch and will purr loudly in response. George is a bit reserved at first but becomes a loyal companion once he feels comfortable. He would be perfect for someone seeking a lowmaintenance, independent cat.
